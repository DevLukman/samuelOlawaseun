Samuel olawaseun

Hey, I'm Samuel Olawaseun a creative photographer based in Kano, nestled in the captivating north east of Nigeria, who believes that photography is the ultimate form of self expression.

It is time where improvisation reigns, allowing me to surrender to the moment and embrace all the elements that surround me. while organization is key in my life, photography offers me freedom to escape into a different world for a few hours.
